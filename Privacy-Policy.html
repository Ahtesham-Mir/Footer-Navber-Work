Privacy Policy

Your trust matters as much as the trails. Here's how we collect, use, and protect your information when you roam with us.

EFFECTIVE: MAY 03, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you explore National Parks with us, we may collect information you provide directly — such as your name, email address, trip preferences, and account details — as well as data gathered automatically, including IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to deliver park guides, personalize trip recommendations, process bookings, send updates about trail conditions and seasonal events, and improve our services. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Cookies & Tracking

Our site uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, analyze traffic patterns, and enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information with trusted partners — park concessionaires, payment processors, and analytics providers — strictly to operate our services. All partners are bound by confidentiality and data-protection agreements.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard safeguards including encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your information. While no system is 100% secure, we work hard to keep your data safe under our canopy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. You may also opt out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our services evolve. Material changes will be posted here with a revised effective date.

9. Contact Us

Questions, requests, or concerns? Reach our team at privacy@nationalparks.example — we typically respond within 3 business days.
