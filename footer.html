KARAKORUM

The Karakoram Range is home to K2, the world's second-highest peak. A haven for mountaineers and trekkers seeking the ultimate challenge in raw, unspoiled terrain.

Explore →

Hunza Valley's terraced farms and ancient forts sit beneath a crown of glaciers, offering a rare blend of culture and wilderness found nowhere else on Earth.

The Fairy Meadows, nestled below Nanga Parbat, offer camping grounds with one of the most dramatic mountain backdrops on the planet — untouched and unforgettable.
